Article Submission Rules
General Guidelines:
  • Articles must be original research or review papers
  • Only PDF format is accepted
  • Maximum file size: 16MB
  • Articles must be properly formatted and cited
  • Plagiarism will result in immediate rejection
Review Process:
  • All submissions undergo peer review
  • Review typically takes 5-10 business days
  • Authors will be notified of decisions via email
  • Rejected articles can be revised and resubmitted
