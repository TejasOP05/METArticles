About Centre of Pharmacy Practice Excellence

Empowering academic excellence through collaborative research sharing and knowledge exchange.

Our Mission

To create a comprehensive platform that facilitates the sharing, discovery, and collaboration of academic research, fostering innovation and knowledge exchange within the academic community.

Our Vision

To be the leading platform for academic research sharing, where students, researchers, and educators come together to advance knowledge and drive meaningful change in their respective fields.

Platform Features

Peer Review Process

All submissions undergo rigorous peer review by qualified academic supervisors to ensure quality and integrity.

Role-Based Access

Tailored experiences for students, authors, and supervisors with appropriate permissions and functionalities.

Advanced Search

Powerful search and filtering capabilities to help users discover relevant research across multiple categories.

How It Works

1
Register

Create an account as an author to start sharing your research with the community.

2
Submit

Upload your research article with detailed metadata for proper categorization and discovery.

3
Review

Academic supervisors review submissions for quality, originality, and academic standards.

4
Publish

Approved articles are published and made available for the global academic community.

User Roles

Students
  • Browse approved articles
  • Download research papers
  • Search by category and keywords
  • No registration required
Authors
  • Submit research articles
  • Track submission status
  • View download statistics
  • Manage article portfolio
Supervisors
  • Review submitted articles
  • Approve or reject submissions
  • Provide feedback to authors
  • Monitor platform quality
